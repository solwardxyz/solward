The gap

Launching a token takes minutes. Proving you built it takes nothing — because there's no way to.

Launchpads removed the capital barrier. The bottleneck moved to credible contribution, and nobody built the rail for it.

01

Contributors are invisible

Ship a critical PR, design a brand, write the thread that drives a launch — none of it follows you. Your reputation lives in scattered Discord roles and screenshots.

02

Projects can't find or trust talent

A founder fresh off a Kickstart launch has an idea and a token, but no reliable way to find a vetted contributor, prove the work, or pay for outcomes.

03

Rewards are arbitrary

Airdrops reward wallets, not work. Bounties get gamed. Grant committees move slowly. The value actually created is never priced.

04

The launchpad era made it worse

Thousands of ideas launch frictionlessly. Almost none can mobilize and reward the talent that would make them real.

The protocol

Three layers that turn work into a record you own.

An open primitive at the base, a marketplace in the middle, compounding rewards on top.

Layer 1

Proof of Contribution

An open standard for attesting work on Solana. Every merged PR, published post, design delivery, or community milestone becomes a soulbound attestation tied to your wallet — issued by projects, peers, or automated oracles, and weighted by the issuer's own reputation. The result is a portable, composable reputation graph any app can read.

soulbound attestationsgithub + content oraclesopen on-chain graph
Layer 2

The Contribution Market

Projects post outcome-based bounties escrowed in USDC or their own token. Contributors apply with their Solward reputation as their resume — no cold DMs. On delivery, work is verified, the attestation mints, and escrow releases automatically. Reputation and payment settle in the same transaction.

outcome bountiesauto-escrowreputation-gated access
Layer 3

Reputation Rewards

Projects and ecosystem funds run retroactive reward rounds that pay the highest-impact contributors — scored objectively by the graph, not a closed committee. Your public Solward profile unlocks gated bounties, better rates, allowlists, and voting weight across any project that reads it.

Why Solana, why now

This only works here — and the timing only works now.

⚡The launchpad needs a labor layer

Kickstart makes ideas instantly tradable. The natural next question — who builds the idea, and how do they get paid? — is exactly what Solward answers. Every launch becomes fundable with talent, not just capital.

◇Composable, not a walled garden

The reputation graph is open on-chain data. Jupiter, Drift, Superteam, hackathon organizers, and any future app can read it. Solward grows the whole ecosystem instead of fencing off a corner.

⏱Only viable at Solana speed & cost

Sub-cent fees and sub-second finality make it economical to attest thousands of micro-contributions and settle micro-rewards — impossible on slower, costlier chains.

▲Rides the institutional wave

As Mastercard, Worldpay, and Western Union build on the Solana Developer Platform, verifiable contributor reputation becomes infrastructure they need too: who can they trust to build, and how is it proven?

Governance

Access
