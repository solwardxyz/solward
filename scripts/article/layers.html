THE PROTOCOL

Three layers that turn work into a record you own

Layer 1

Proof of Contribution

An open standard for attesting work on Solana. Every merged PR, post, design, or milestone becomes a soulbound attestation — a portable reputation graph any app can read.

soulbound attestationsgithub + content oraclesopen on-chain graph
Layer 2

The Contribution Market

Projects post outcome-based bounties escrowed in USDC or their token. Contributors apply with reputation as their resume. On delivery, work is verified, the attestation mints, and escrow releases — in one transaction.

outcome bountiesauto-escrowreputation-gated
Layer 3

Reputation Rewards

Retroactive reward rounds pay the highest-impact contributors, scored by the graph — not a closed committee. Your public profile unlocks gated bounties, better rates, and voting weight across the ecosystem.
